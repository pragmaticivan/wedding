The Proposal -

June 12th was a special day for me. In Brazil, we celebrate valentine's day. I told Gabriella we were going to a double date to celebrate that special day. So, I invited Zeno and Carol to help him with that journey.

Gabriella always told me that if one day I do that, I should make sure she would get her nails done. YAY.

Life was not meant to be enjoyed alone and there would be no reason to not celebrate this moment with friends. So, I invited a couple of people she would like to have around on that special moment. And also asked her family for some help, because I was sure I would not be able to do everything alone.

If you know me well, you will see that I'm very low key on planning events, but I really wanted to do something memorable and special for the woman I love.

I planned all details and after dinner I took her back home where I got everything prepared with everyone's help.

You would never be prepared for a moment like that, so I was shaking. But can surely say that was one of the best days of my life. To declare my love for her and make sure I would have her by my side for the rest of my life.

Everythings was settled up like I imagined. Lots of cancdles, red flowers, a handwrite drawing I made a couple of days before and friends and family to share this moment with us.

She said yes!

Ivan Santos
